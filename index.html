About Me

My Name is Ryan Burns and I am a Full Stack Developer in training. I was born in Orlando Florida on June 17th 1993 making me one of very few Florida Natives.

My interest in becoming a developer began my freshman year of college. I was one of those students that would never go to class if I could get away with not doing so. Unfortunately, like most students who take this approach, I began to miss important lectures and as a result had literally no useful notes when it came time to study for exams. Rather than just doing what was expected of me and going to class, I began to brainstorm on loopholes instead. In my mind there had to be some way that I could skip class and still have pages full of notes at the ready when I needed them. That very next day I began illustrating an app that could solve my problems. I drew out what each screen would look, how users would interact with eachother and the site itself and even how to monetize my creation.

The next step was clear...I had to learn how to build this thing. I had no idea what I was getting myself into. I tried numerous times to teach myself basic html, css and javascript. I struggled with the amount of content out there and quickly became overwhelmed by the task ahead of me. I put the idea, and the task of learning to code on the backburner and proceeded with my life for the next 7 years. Now here we are 7 years later, I've enrolled in the UCF Coding Bootcamp and have committed myself to learning how to be a developer.... and yes, I stil plan on building my App!
